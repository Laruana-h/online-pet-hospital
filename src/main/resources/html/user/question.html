Why do dogs like to bite?

Puppy after weaning, about a month old, is in long teeth, teeth will itch; To four or five years of age, because of tooth replacement, will also be very itchy;

At least until the age of eight or nine, such as permanent teeth completely stop growing, slowly no longer itching.

During this time, they will bite to relieve the itching. You don't bite when you're an adult.

Why do dogs like chasing cars?

This is a dog's nature and a bad habit that can be corrected.

Dogs are natural hunters and like to chase moving prey, not only cars and pedestrians, but also others, such as dogs, cats, birds, flies, cockroaches and so on.
